list of rare animals in Indonesia

According to government regulation number 27 of 1999 concerning the types of plants and animals that are protected. That there are actually thousands of species of flora and fauna that live in Indonesia and there are 294 species of Indonesian flora and fauna that are classified as endangered species and must be protected. On this occasion it will be explained about 30 species of animals that are classified as rare and enter the critical zone. So it needs to be done various efforts to keep it in the area of conservation or protection of animals. There is no term rare animal but there is a term "endangered animal" which is commonly used by a number of international conservation agencies or organizations. For example, the International Union for the Conservation of Nature and Natural Resources (IUCN). An international institution engaged actively in dealing with various natural resource problems including flora and fauna. Especially for animals that have been included in the endangered list or red list of threatened species. In this case the organization cooperates with various countries in the world.

Bird of paradise with a native habitat in the lowlands is a bird that becomes the mascot of papua island. Divided into 41 species, and spread in West Papua as many as 38 species, on the island of Torres to eastern Australia. The most known types of Cendrawasih include the Great Yellow Cendrawasih or Cendrawasih Paradise Apoda. Birds of paradise itself have a different size depending on the type of species that is divided into species King Bird of Paradise (15 cm in size) to black sicklebill species (110 cm or 1 meter in size). The main characteristic of the bird of paradise lies in the color of its feathers that attract attention such as red, green, and blue, based on this name also the naming of this bird for example in the yellow paradise that is more dominant in its yellow color, the dominant red paradise red color, green and blue paradise as well.

Komodo

Komodo, as the first rare animal in Indonesia is also the only ancient animal that still lives today. This rare animal has another name Varatus Komodoensis or Orah is also one of the largest reptile species in the world, Komodo dragons have a very strong bite and can be very deadly, this venomous poison comes from thousands of glands in the gum area. Komodo habitat itself can currently only be found on Komodo Island, Flores, Gili Matang, Gili Dasami and Rinca East Nusa Tenggara Indonesia. Komodo has now become an animal protected by the Indonesian government with the construction of Komodo National Park.

Harimau Sumatera

The Sumatran tiger or in Latin Panthera Tigris Sondaica is a rare endangered species of animal. Living in endemic native Sumatra Island, until now only about 500 Sumatran tigers remain this is caused by habitat damage to high mortality (So far recorded is 66 Sumatran tigers killed between 1998 and 2000). Not until there sumatran tigers are also often circulated in the illegal trade where body parts are traded at high prices on the black market, namely to be used as jewelry, leather bags, belts and much more.
